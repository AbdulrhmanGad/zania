Overview

Easily manage and keep track of all project management activities for your construction projects through our Construction Management System. Fully integrated with Sales, Billing, Accounting and Budgeting, Inventory and Tasks.

Construction Made Easy !

  • Automatically create a project with parent-tasks and sub-tasks based on your quotation or quotation template.
  • Send clear and complete quotations to your prospects. Add service descriptions, and additional information simply by dragging and dropping building blocks from your project template.
  • Record and keep track of all material consumed for a given project in the related tasks.
  • Integration with inventory with stock moves automatically generated and validated based the material listed within a task.
  • Direct material allocation to a project (where stock is moved from a vendor location to the client location through a transit location tracked by the system).
  • Allocate resources and keep track of your payments to your resources linked with budgeting and accounting.
  • Easily keep track of your equipment’s used in your project by just adding them into a task.
  • Neat and efficient overview of your projects.
  • Get the most efficient stocking method and improve all your internal operations double-entry inventory has no stock input, output or transformation. Instead, all operations are stock moves between locations.
  • Generate bills based on equipment, resources, subcontracts direct from billings and they automatically get populated in tasks.
  • Automatic journal entry creation and validation.
  • Integration with Budgeting with easy budget tracking.
  • Create and keep track of sub-contracts for a given project.
  • Keep track of your projects, revenue and expenses through our detailed and visually custom dashboard.
