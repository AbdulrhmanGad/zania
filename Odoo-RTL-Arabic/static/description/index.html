Odoo Arabic Right to Left

This module provides right to left direction to arabic language

First, you have to install the module.

Apps > Odoo Arabic Right to Left

then, you have to change your language to arabic language.

Administrator > Preferences

Change your language to arabic and then click save.

Your language now is arabic.
